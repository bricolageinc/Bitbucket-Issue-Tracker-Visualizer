Instructions:
  1. In BitBucket, Go to Settings -> Import & Export.
  2. Click on "Start Export".
  3. Unzip your zip file, and open the JSON in a text editor.
  4. Paste it into the box on this page and click "Load JSON".
  5. Once it's loaded, you can export to Excel.
